ISQCMC ’25 : 3rd International Symposium on Quantum Computing and Musical Creativity

ISQCMC '25

Palermo, Complesso monumentale dello Steri, sala delle Capriate, University of Palermo, Italy
October 28-29, 2025

GUIDELINES

Paper Presentations

Slides will be run from one only computer. Please send us slides and audio files in advance.

    - 15 minutes Presentation + 5 minutes for Questions
    - Stereo sound and standard projection (let us know if sounds will be played)
    - There are no breaks between presentations, please respect the time limits
    - During questions time the next presenter should get ready to present
    - Wi-Fi is not guaranteed. Please do not to rely on online material

Music Presentations

The presentations will be run from one only computer. If the participants needs to play an audio file, same. If there is a live performance that does not require computer connection or projector, no problem. If a performance requires a connection to the audio/video system, this can be done via HDMI, with the signal possibly sent to the main computer. If a separate computer has to be used, please let us know. Please do not rely on Wi-Fi, as a guest account is not guaranteed.

    - Your should aim the length of your music presentation to about 10 minutes
    - You can briefly talk about your work (up to 5 minutes, or more if work < 10 min)
    - You should bring all the required equipment, instruments, and performers
    - You can connect to room stereo sound system (via mini jack, ¼’’ TRS, or XLR)
    - You can also connect to the standard projection
    - Between each presentation there will be only 5 minutes to swap
    - Before each block of music presentations there will be a 40 minute break that you can use for setup and soundcheck.
    - If you have any questions about your setup please contact us asap


There is a cafeteria inside the Botanical Garden, near Sala Lanza. The participants can freely access it. However, the coffee break is not offered. If any changes occur, we will notify the participants at the conference.
